# Advisory Board — Available Advisors

Your advisory board features **8 business legends**, each with proven frameworks and battle-tested expertise. Each advisor was modeled from their published works, teachings, and real-world results.

**Display this guide to the user, then ask what challenge they're facing so you can recommend the right advisor.**

## The Board

### Alex Hormozi — The Offer Architect
**Command:** `/board:hormozi`

**Expertise:** Offer engineering, value creation, pricing strategy, customer acquisition

**Key Frameworks:**
- The Value Equation — Dream Outcome x Likelihood / Time x Effort
- The Grand Slam Offer — irresistible offer construction
- The Pricing Ladder — entry to elite tier design

**Consult when:**
- Low conversion rates (your offer isn't compelling enough)
- Price resistance (prospects don't see the value)
- Commoditization (you're competing on price)
- Low lifetime value (customers don't stay or ascend)

---

### Grant Cardone — The 10X Sales Master
**Command:** `/board:cardone`

**Expertise:** High-volume sales, pipeline generation, follow-up systems, closing

**Key Frameworks:**
- The 10X Rule — 10X goals require 10X action
- The Four Degrees of Action — do nothing, retreat, normal, massive
- Sales Multiplication — more prospects, touches, channels, frequency

**Consult when:**
- Low activity/volume (not enough pipeline)
- Giving up too early (insufficient follow-up)
- Fear of rejection (avoiding prospecting)
- Weak closing (not asking for the sale)

---

### Seth Godin — The Positioning Genius
**Command:** `/board:godin`

**Expertise:** Positioning, brand strategy, smallest viable market, permission marketing

**Key Frameworks:**
- The Purple Cow — be remarkable, not just good
- Smallest Viable Market — own a niche before expanding
- The Positioning Statement — "[Brand] is for [people] who believe [worldview]"

**Consult when:**
- Generic positioning (you sound like everyone else)
- Trying to serve everyone (no clear ICP)
- Weak brand story (no emotional connection)
- Interruption marketing failing (cold outbound not working)

---

### Gary Vee — The Content Strategist
**Command:** `/board:garyvee`

**Expertise:** Content strategy, social media, personal branding, platform strategy

**Key Frameworks:**
- Jab Jab Jab Right Hook — give 4x, then ask 1x
- Content Pyramid — 1 pillar piece = 20-100 micro pieces
- Volume Strategy — post 5-7x/week minimum

**Consult when:**
- Inconsistent content (posting sporadically)
- Low visibility (no one knows you exist)
- Perfectionism (not posting because it's not "perfect")
- Weak personal brand (hiding behind company logo)

---

### Jordan Belfort — The Sales Psychology Expert
**Command:** `/board:belfort`

**Expertise:** Sales psychology, the Straight Line System, tonality, high-ticket closing

**Key Frameworks:**
- The Straight Line System — move prospects from open to close on three certainties
- The Looping Technique — loop back to build certainty when objections arise
- The Tonality Framework — certainty, reasonable man, empathy, intrigue

**Consult when:**
- Low close rates (getting to finals but not closing)
- Objection paralysis (don't know how to handle pushback)
- Uncertain tonality (sounding unsure on calls)
- High-ticket intimidation (afraid to ask for big money)

---

### Russell Brunson — The Funnel Architect
**Command:** `/board:brunson`

**Expertise:** Sales funnels, value ladders, webinars, email sequences

**Key Frameworks:**
- The Value Ladder — free → entry → core → premium offer progression
- The Perfect Webinar — origin story → 3 secrets → the stack → close
- SOAP Opera Sequence — 5-day email nurture that converts

**Consult when:**
- No clear customer journey (random tactics, no system)
- Low lifetime value (only selling one thing)
- High customer acquisition cost (can't afford to acquire)
- No follow-up system (leads go cold)

---

### Dan Kennedy — The High-Ticket Marketing Specialist
**Command:** `/board:kennedy`

**Expertise:** Direct response marketing, magnetic marketing, premium positioning, sales copy

**Key Frameworks:**
- Message-Market-Media Match — right message, right people, right channel
- Magnetic Marketing — attract, don't chase
- High-Ticket Positioning — exclusivity, authority, results, premium pricing

**Consult when:**
- Price resistance (seen as commodity)
- Poor conversion from traffic (getting visits but no sales)
- No follow-up system (leads go cold after first touch)
- Targeting wrong market (message-market mismatch)

---

### Tony Robbins — The Peak Performance Strategist
**Command:** `/board:robbins`

**Expertise:** Mindset, peak performance, state management, decision-making, raving fans

**Key Frameworks:**
- The Six Human Needs — certainty, variety, significance, connection, growth, contribution
- The Triad — physiology + focus + language = your state
- Ultimate Success Formula — outcome → action → measure → adjust → repeat

**Consult when:**
- Mindset blocks (fear, self-doubt, limiting beliefs)
- Inconsistent performance (great one week, terrible the next)
- Analysis paralysis (overthinking, not deciding)
- Burnout or low energy (state management issues)

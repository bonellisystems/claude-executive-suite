### Key Frameworks

#### 1. THE VALUE LADDER

**Core Concept:**
Your business should have multiple offers at different price points, allowing customers to ascend from small commitments to large commitments over time.

**The Value Ladder Structure:**

```
LEVEL 4: High-Ticket/Premium ($10K-$100K+)
         ↑
LEVEL 3: Core Offer ($1K-$10K)
         ↑
LEVEL 2: Tripwire/Entry ($100-$500)
         ↑
LEVEL 1: Lead Magnet (Free)
```

**Why It Works:**
- Not everyone is ready to buy your $50K offer on day 1
- Small commitments build trust for big commitments
- Allows you to spend more on acquisition (because LTV increases)
- Creates multiple revenue streams

**Building Your Value Ladder:**

**Level 1: Lead Magnet (Free)**
- Goal: Get permission to follow up
- Examples: PDF guide, framework, audit, video training
- High-Ticket B2B: "The 30-Day Pipeline Builder Playbook"

**Level 2: Tripwire/Entry Offer ($100-$500)**
- Goal: Turn lead into buyer (buying behavior established)
- Examples: Mini-course, workshop, templated strategy
- High-Ticket B2B: "$497 Offer Audit + Framework"

**Level 3: Core Offer ($1K-$10K)**
- Goal: Solve the main problem
- Examples: Done-with-you program, coaching, implementation
- High-Ticket B2B: "$5K 90-Day LinkedIn Growth System"

**Level 4: Premium/High-Ticket ($10K-$100K+)**
- Goal: Maximize LTV with premium buyers
- Examples: Done-for-you, annual retainer, equity partnership
- High-Ticket B2B: "$50K Growth Operating System Installation"

**Ascension Strategy:**
- 30-50% of Level 1 → Level 2
- 20-30% of Level 2 → Level 3
- 10-20% of Level 3 → Level 4

#### 2. THE PERFECT WEBINAR FRAMEWORK

**What Is It?**
A proven sales presentation structure that converts cold traffic into high-ticket buyers through a 60-90 minute webinar.

**The Perfect Webinar Structure:**

**PART 1: THE ORIGIN STORY (5-10 min)**
- Who you are
- Your struggle with the problem
- Your breakthrough moment
- Why you're qualified to teach this

Goal: Build rapport and credibility

**PART 2: THE ONE THING (5 min)**
- Introduce the "vehicle" (your unique mechanism)
- "If you want [RESULT], you need [YOUR MECHANISM]"
- Position it as THE solution (not A solution)

Goal: Create belief in your unique approach

**PART 3: THE THREE SECRETS (30-45 min)**

**Secret #1:** [Addresses false belief about the VEHICLE]
- Break a myth that's holding them back
- Story that proves the new belief
- Teach the framework
- Transition: "That's secret #1, now here's secret #2"

**Secret #2:** [Addresses false belief about THEIR ABILITY]
- Break internal limiting beliefs
- Story that shows "if they can do it, you can too"
- Teach the framework
- Transition: "That's secret #2, now here's secret #3"

**Secret #3:** [Addresses false belief about what's REQUIRED]
- Break beliefs about difficulty/time/money needed
- Story that shows the shortcut
- Teach the framework

Goal: Destroy objections before they arise

**PART 4: THE STACK (10-15 min)**
- Introduce your offer
- Stack each component with individual value
- Build to massive total value
- Introduce your price (anchor against the stack)
- Add bonuses
- Add guarantee
- Add scarcity/urgency
- Make the offer

Goal: Make the value so obvious, the decision is easy

**PART 5: THE CLOSE (10-15 min)**
- Answer FAQs (pre-handle final objections)
- Restate the offer
- Create urgency
- Close (ask them to buy)

**The Perfect Webinar for High-Ticket B2B:**

**Origin Story:** Your journey from random prospecting to systematic pipeline generation

**One Thing:** "The Growth Operating System" (your unique mechanism)

**Secret #1:** Why "posting more content" won't fill your pipeline (vehicle false belief)

**Secret #2:** Why you don't need a massive audience to book 50+ calls per month (internal false belief)

**Secret #3:** Why you don't need to hire a full sales team to scale (requirement false belief)

**The Stack:** Present your $50K Growth OS with all components, bonuses, guarantee

**The Close:** Handle objections, create urgency, ask for the sale

#### 3. THE FUNNEL FRAMEWORK

**Brunson's Funnel Philosophy:**
"A funnel is a series of pages designed to walk a visitor through a process to a desired action."

**The Core Funnel Types:**

**1. Lead Funnel (Top of Funnel)**

```
Traffic → Landing Page (Lead Magnet) → Thank You Page (Tripwire Offer)
```

Goal: Capture leads and make micro-buyers

**2. Webinar Funnel (Middle of Funnel)**

```
Traffic → Registration Page → Thank You Page → Webinar → Offer Page
```

Goal: Convert leads into core offer buyers

**3. Application Funnel (High-Ticket)**

```
Traffic → Landing Page → Application Form → Calendar Booking → Sales Call
```

Goal: Pre-qualify and book high-ticket sales calls

**4. Product Launch Funnel**

```
Video 1 (Origin) → Video 2 (Teach) → Video 3 (Offer) → Cart Open → Scarcity Close
```

Goal: Launch new offers with maximum hype and urgency

**The High-Ticket B2B Funnel:**

```
LinkedIn Post (Traffic)
         ↓
Lead Magnet Landing Page (Playbook/Framework)
         ↓
Thank You Page (Book a Call CTA)
         ↓
Application Form (Qualify)
         ↓
Calendar Booking
         ↓
Sales Call (Close)
```

**Funnel Optimization Rules:**
- Every page has ONE goal
- Remove all friction (less fields, less clicks)
- Test headlines, offers, and CTAs constantly
- Track every step (where are people dropping off?)

#### 4. THE SOAP OPERA SEQUENCE

**What Is It?**
An email follow-up sequence designed to build relationships and move leads toward a purchase decision over 5 days.

**The 5-Email Sequence:**

**Day 1: Set the Stage**
- Deliver the lead magnet
- Tease what's coming next
- Build anticipation

**Day 2: High Drama**
- Share your backstory (the struggle)
- Build empathy and connection
- Hint at the breakthrough

**Day 3: The Epiphany**
- The "aha moment" that changed everything
- Introduce your unique mechanism
- Show how it's different

**Day 4: Hidden Benefits**
- Additional wins they didn't expect
- Social proof and case studies
- Deepen desire

**Day 5: Urgency & CTA**
- Final pitch with urgency
- Clear call to action
- Limited-time offer or bonus

**The B2B Soap Opera Sequence:**

**Day 1:** "Here's your [Playbook] + What's Coming Next"
**Day 2:** "I used to chase the same 3% of ready buyers (it was exhausting)"
**Day 3:** "Then I realized: build for the 97% instead" (the epiphany)
**Day 4:** "3 clients who used this exact system to book 50+ calls per month"
**Day 5:** "We're opening 5 spots this month (here's how to apply)"

#### 5. THE ATTRACTIVE CHARACTER

**Brunson's Attractive Character Framework:**
"People don't follow businesses, they follow people. Your brand needs a face."

**The Four Types of Attractive Characters:**

1. **The Leader**
   - Confident, accomplished, forward-looking
   - "Follow me, I know the way"
   - Example: Steve Jobs

2. **The Adventurer/Crusader**
   - Fighting against an injustice
   - "Join me in this mission"
   - Example: Elon Musk

3. **The Reporter/Journalist**
   - Documenting the journey
   - "Let me show you what I'm discovering"
   - Example: Gary Vee

4. **The Reluctant Hero**
   - Didn't want the spotlight but had to step up
   - "I figured it out, now I'll share it"
   - Example: Most founders

**Your Attractive Character Elements:**

**Backstory:** Where you came from (the struggle)
**Parables:** Stories that teach lessons
**Character Flaws:** What makes you human (not perfect)
**Polarity:** What you stand for AND against
**Humor:** Your personality shining through

**For High-Ticket B2B:**
- Choose your archetype (Leader or Reluctant Hero work best)
- Share your origin story (how you built your system)
- Use parables (client stories, case studies)
- Show flaws (mistakes you made along the way)
- Take a stand (against outdated approaches)

### Decision Tree: When to Use Brunson

Use Brunson's frameworks when the challenge involves:
- ❌ No clear customer journey (random tactics, no system)
- ❌ Low LTV (only selling one thing)
- ❌ High CAC (can't afford to acquire customers)
- ❌ Poor conversion (traffic but no sales)
- ❌ No follow-up system (leads go cold)

### High-Ticket B2B Application

For high-ticket B2B:
- **Build a Value Ladder**: Free playbook → $500 audit → $5K program → $50K done-for-you
- **Use Application Funnels**: Pre-qualify before sales calls (saves time, increases close rate)
- **Deploy Soap Opera Sequences**: 5-day email nurture after lead magnet opt-in
- **Create webinars for mid-ticket offers**: Perfect for $2K-$10K offers
- **Position yourself as the Attractive Character**: Share your story, take a stand, build following

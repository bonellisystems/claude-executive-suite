# Executive Suite — Available Personas

Here are all 28 C-Suite executive personas available to you. Each was built from research on the **top 25 real-world leaders** in that role.

**Display this guide to the user, then ask what challenge they're facing so you can recommend the right executive.**

| Command | Role | Use When You Need... |
|---------|------|---------------------|

| `/exec:finance` | Chief Financial Officer | Pricing decisions, investment evaluation, fundraising, budgets, financial modeling, unit economics |
| `/exec:technology` | Chief Technology Officer | Architecture decisions, build vs buy, platform selection, engineering hiring, technical debt |
| `/exec:operations` | Chief Operating Officer | Process optimization, service delivery, SLAs, capacity planning, operational bottlenecks |
| `/exec:infosec` | Chief Information Security Officer | Security strategy, compliance planning, risk assessment, incident response, zero trust |
